# Deep Strategy: Finding South African C-Levels in Dubai/UAE Companies

*Goal: Identify UAE-based companies with C-level executives originally from South Africa → these companies almost certainly hire contractors/freelancers in South Africa.*

**This is our #1 corridor: 18.2% conversion rate from Qatar→SA campaign (6 qualified from 33 replies).**

---

## Why Dubai + South Africa

- **Proven**: 18.2% conversion — best of ALL EasyStaff Global corridors
- Massive SA diaspora in Dubai: ~100,000+ South Africans live in UAE
- SA professionals dominate Dubai's finance, tech, hospitality, real estate, and consulting sectors
- Common pattern: SA exec moves to Dubai tax-free → keeps team/contractors back in SA
- ZAR weakness makes SA contractors extremely affordable for AED-paying companies
- Time zone overlap: UAE (GMT+4) and SA (GMT+2) = 2 hours difference, perfect for real-time work
- English is primary business language in both markets
- Existing wins: NEOM (4 contacts), Digital Bridge, Tracking, Pioneers Consulting

## 4. Dubai/UAE-Specific Search Strategies

### Strategy 1: Dubai Free Zone Companies

Dubai free zones are where most SA-led companies register. Target companies in:
- **DIFC** (Dubai International Financial Centre) — finance, fintech
- **DMCC** (Dubai Multi Commodities Centre) — trading, commodities
- **Dubai Internet City** — tech companies
- **Dubai Media City** — media, marketing
- **Dubai Silicon Oasis** — tech startups
- **JAFZA** (Jebel Ali Free Zone) — logistics, manufacturing
- **Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM)** — finance

**Apollo filter**: Company HQ = United Arab Emirates + [university/surname filters]

### Strategy 2: SA Professional Networks in Dubai

**LinkedIn Groups to mine for leads:**
- "South Africans in Dubai" (~50K+ members)
- "South African Professionals in the UAE"
- "SA Expats Dubai"
- "SABC Dubai" (South African Business Council)
- "Southern Africa Business Forum UAE"

**How to use**: Join groups → export member list (SalesNav) → filter by C-level title → enrich emails in Apollo/Clay

### Strategy 3: SA Schools in Dubai (reverse signal)

Dubai has SA-curriculum schools. Parents at these schools = SA expats in leadership roles:
- South African International School (SAIS)
- Curro Dubai
- Reddam House (SA school chain)

Companies sponsoring these schools or advertising there = SA-led businesses.
