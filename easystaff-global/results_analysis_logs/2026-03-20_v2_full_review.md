## TOP 3 ISSUES TO FIX

### 1. GEOGRAPHY FILTER MISSING
6 non-UAE companies passed analysis. The prompt doesn't check geography.
**Fix:** Add to prompt: "Company MUST be based in or have office in UAE. Non-UAE domains (.in, .ir, .sd, .com.au) without clear UAE presence = NOT_A_MATCH."

### 2. SOLO CONSULTANTS PASSING
Individual consultants (1-person advisory) are NOT targets — they don't hire freelancers.
**Fix:** Add to exclusion rules: "Solo consultants, individual advisors, personal websites with one person = NOT_A_MATCH. We need companies with TEAMS (5+ people)."

### 3. DUPLICATES
26 duplicate entries from multiple gathering runs analyzing the same DiscoveredCompany.
**Fix:** Dedup analysis_results by discovered_company_id. Only keep the latest analysis per company.

## PROMPT V3 SUGGESTIONS
Add these lines to the via negativa exclusions:
```
GEOGRAPHY (always NOT_A_MATCH):
- Company not based in UAE and no UAE office/presence
- Domains like .in, .ir, .pk, .com.au without clear UAE address
- Website in non-English/non-Arabic without UAE mentions

SOLO/TINY (always NOT_A_MATCH):
- Solo consultant, individual advisor, personal website
- Companies with obviously 1-2 people and no team
```
