### Q: "What tangible results will your customers experience?"
> Increase LTV & AOV: 70% of sellers prefer brand credit payouts and tend to spend 2.5x more on subsequent purchases in your main e-shop.
> Attract New Customers: 40% of resale platform users are new to the brand.
> Monetize Unsold Inventory: Convert past collections, overstocks, and returns into revenue.
> Stronger Community: Engage with your customers' post-purchase experience.
> X$ revenue brought back from 3rd party marketplaces = branded resale channel adds up to 15% revenue without extra operations or workload

### Q: "Can you explain it here?"
> Hi,
> I'd be happy to share more details. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call? I can also prepare a short analysis of your brand's current resale activity and show you how a branded resale platform could look for [Company].
> Here's the link to my calendar: [calendar link]
> Looking forward to speaking soon!

### Q: "Can you send more info to the email"
> Hello,
> Thank you for your response!
> I've attached a PDF with more information about how our solution works, examples of brands we already work with, and the benefits they've seen.
> Also, if you find it interesting, I'd be happy to arrange a short call to answer any questions.

### Q: "We don't have overstock" (objection handling)
> Just to clarify — we don't resell old stock ourselves. Instead, we help brands launch a branded resale platform where your own customers can resell their used items, and you earn a commission.
> Here's a calculator: [link]
> Here's the website: thefashionpeople.com
