## A/B Testing Protocol (from AFFPERF, applies to all)

- Timing: Touch 1 → Touch 2: 3 biz days, Touch 2 → Touch 3: 5 biz days, after Touch 3: LinkedIn
- Cohort: Max 50 contacts per send, min 100 per variant to declare winner
- Week 1-2: Soft CTA vs Direct CTA → reply rate + booked calls
- Week 3-4: Winning CTA → subject line variants → open rate
- Week 5-6: Winning subject → angle variants → reply-to-meeting %
