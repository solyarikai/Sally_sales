## Segment 3 — IM_FIRST_AGENCIES

Agencies where influencer marketing is the core business (not a side service). 10-500 employees.

> Validated by Sally's data: Brighter Click (Founder, 10-50 emp) = strong fit. GameInfluencer (CEO, 50-200 emp) = engaged.
> NOT validated: marketing holdings (WPP, Omnicom, HAVAS, Mindshare = 0 conversions from Sally's data, only OOO replies). Micro-agencies <10 emp (BrandNation 2-10 = "not a fit", Yagency = "too small, cannot afford"). PR agencies (0.20% reply, 0 meetings).

### Company filters

**Industry**
```
Marketing & Advertising
```

> Intentionally narrow — only Marketing & Advertising. No "Public Relations & Communications" (PR firms = 0 conversions in Sally's data).

**Company Keywords — ANY of**
```
influencer marketing agency, influencer agency,
creator agency, influencer management, creator campaigns,
influencer marketing, creator partnerships,
TikTok agency, influencer talent, creator talent,
influencer strategy, UGC agency
```

> Key change from v2: REMOVED "social media agency", "content marketing agency", "social media marketing", "Instagram marketing". These keywords pull thousands of generic SMM agencies that don't do influencer marketing. Sally's data showed these never converted.

**Excluded Company Keywords**
```
SEO agency, PPC agency, web design, software development,
recruitment, HR, staffing, healthcare, legal, accounting,
logistics, manufacturing, real estate, fintech, insurance,
construction, education, nonprofit, government, defense,
food service, restaurant, hospitality, travel agency,
freelance, solo consultant, print, media buying only,
PR agency, public relations, crisis communications,
web development, app development, branding only,
market research, consulting firm, management consulting
```

> Explicit exclusions for PR, consulting, branding-only, and market research — all tested by Sally with 0 results.

**# Employees:** 10–500

> Lower bound 10 (not 20): Brighter Click = 10-50 and was a strong fit.
> Upper bound 500: no marketing holdings. Mindshare (10K+), HAVAS, WPP = 0 conversions.

**Location (priority order)**
```
United States, United Kingdom, Germany, Netherlands, France,
Australia, Canada, Spain, Belgium, Denmark
```

> Removed from priority: Sweden (BrandNation, Yagency both "not a fit" — too small market, micro-agencies), Italy (no conversions in Sally's data).
> Kept Australia: HAVAS Red AU showed engagement but was OOO. Worth testing.

### People filters

**Job Titles**
```
CEO, Founder, Co-Founder, Managing Director, Managing Partner,
Head of Influencer Marketing, Director of Influencer,

Director of Client Services, Head of Strategy,
```

## Filter validation checklist

Before launching any segment, verify with a 25-company sample:

- [ ] Scrape 25 websites from the Apollo export
- [ ] Manually check: does this company actually do what the segment describes?
- [ ] If <70% match — tighten keywords or add exclusions
- [ ] If >90% match — filters are good, proceed
- [ ] Cross-check against Sally's successful leads — would they have been caught by these filters?
- [ ] Cross-check against Sally's failures — would they have been excluded?
