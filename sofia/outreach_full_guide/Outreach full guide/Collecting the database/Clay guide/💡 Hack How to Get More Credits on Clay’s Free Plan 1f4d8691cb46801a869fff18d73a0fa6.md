# 💡 Hack: How to Get More Credits on Clay’s Free Plan

Here’s a quick tip for anyone running low on Clay credits but needs to finish tasks fast — like finding emails or completing other important jobs.

### How it works:

1. Message Clay support asking for extra credits before buying a subscription. For example:

```

Hi,

Could you please provide me with a small amount of credits to try out your service before I purchase a subscription?

I’d really appreciate it, especially since my previous credits were accidentally spent due to the auto-update function.

```

1. The bot will ask if this was an unexpected expense — reply **Yes**.
2. Specify how many credits you need. Some users got 200 instantly and up to 1000 after a short wait with an operator.
3. The bot will send you a guide on how to avoid wasting credits (like turning off Auto Update in some features).

Follow the steps, click “Got it,” and voilà — credits are added, often without human intervention. Use them wisely! 🙌
