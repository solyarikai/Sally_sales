# Filtering by University (Sales Navigator Method)

### 

Another highly effective method for identifying **Russian-speaking contacts** is by filtering through **education history** in **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**.

You can use a list of universities from:

- **Russia**
- **Belarus**
- **Ukraine**
- **Kazakhstan**

This method helps you find founders, CTOs, and other decision-makers who are likely to speak Russian — even if they're currently based abroad.

📌 **Steps:**

1. Go to **Sales Navigator → Lead search**
2. Use the **"University"** filter
3. Paste in the name of a university from the list below

You can also combine this with location, title (e.g. CTO, Co-Founder), and industry filters to make the search even more targeted.
