## What Works

- **Short emails:** 40-65 words optimal (was 80-100 in v2/v3)
- **Pain-based opening** вместо feature dump
- **Dual CTA:** pricing doc vs walkthrough (70% of warm replies ask about pricing)
- **Social proof in Email 2** (region-specific references)
- **White-label positioning** для India — 100% warm rate
- **MENA+APAC sequences** — 2.17% reply rate, 100% warm

## What Doesn't Work

- Feature-dump approach в Email 1
- Identical subject lines across segments
- A/B tests too similar (need more divergence)
- CTA "book a call" only (без pricing option) — теряем 70% interested replies
- Europe campaigns с generic messaging — 0% reply rate
- PR firms segment — dead (0/433 replies)

## Reply Handling (from operator_playbook)

- **36,192 conversations** analyzed
- Response categories: interested (2%), meeting request (3.5%), question (2.2%), not interested (8%), OOO (13.2%), wrong person (18.5%), unsubscribe (1.6%), other (51%)
- **Interested sub-types:** demo requests (37), pricing questions (101), materials (103), positive (63), colleague redirects (41), trial (7)
- **Key rule:** Pricing questions → share framework, redirect to call after understanding usage needs
- **Response SLA:** Meeting request 1h, Interested 2h, Question 4h

## Best Practices (validated)

- 75-100 word max per email (industry benchmark)
- Plain text only
- 4-7 email touchpoints per sequence
- JTBD question should be in Email 1
- 500-email pause rule: auto-pause at 500 sent if 0 replies
- Weekly list cleaning (Fridays)
- MillionVerifier bounce filtering before sends
