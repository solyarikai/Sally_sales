# OnSocial — Market & ICP

## TAM Assessment

- **SaaS platform segment:** 3-6K компаний globally
- **IM-first agencies:** 620-1,040 компаний (highest TAM potential)
- **Total addressable contacts:** ~6,000-12,000 (Clay estimates: 2,970-6,120 contacts)
- ~10K leads уже отправлено — рост ограничен без новых гео/keywords

## ICP by Segment

### INFPLAT (Influencer Platforms)
- **Roles:** CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Product, Co-Founder
- **Company size:** 5-5,000 employees
- **Pain:** Строят data pipeline сами, 2+ инженера тратят время на maintenance, coverage отстает (weekly updates vs 24-48h)
- **Trigger:** Расширяют покрытие платформ или добавляют creator analytics как фичу
- **Seed companies:** Kolsquare, Skeepers, Phyllo, KlugKlug, Qoruz, Favikon, TRIBEGroup

### IMAGENCY (IM-First Agencies)
- **Roles:** CEO, Founder, Managing Director, Head of IM, Head of Partnerships
- **Company size:** 10-500 employees
- **Pain:** Клиенты гуглят vendor price, 67% брендов идут in-house, нужна дифференциация
- **Trigger:** Теряют клиентов, ищут способ повысить retention
- **Seed companies:** Whalar, Billion Dollar Boy, Linkster, Ykone, Intermate, LADbible, InfluencerUK

### AFFPERF (Affiliate & Performance)
- **Roles:** VP Product, Head of Data, CTO
- **Company size:** 20-5,000 employees
- **Pain:** Нет creator performance данных в attribution stack
- **Status:** Сегмент в тестировании, результаты пока слабые

## Competitors

Прямые конкуренты (в blacklist): HypeAuditor, Modash, GRIN.
Позиционирование vs конкурентов: никогда не badmouth, фокус на уникальных differentiators (data freshness, coverage breadth, white-label).

## Geographic Insights

- **India:** White-label positioning resonates strongly. 100% warm rate. Agencies want data "under their brand"
- **MENA+APAC:** 8.7% meeting rate — 10x vs US/EU. Less competition, higher urgency
- **Germany:** UWG compliance risk for cold email — LinkedIn preferred channel
- **US/EU:** Largest TAM but lowest conversion (0-0.05% meeting rate). Saturated market

## Key Market Stats (for sequences)

- 67% of brands going in-house with influencer marketing (agency threat)
- 40% higher retention with branded analytics (agency value prop)
- 4-6 months to build creator data pipeline from scratch (platform value prop)
- 24-48h update frequency vs industry-standard weekly
