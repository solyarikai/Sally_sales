### CORRIDOR SCORING FOR BLACKLIST PRIORITIZATION

When selecting contacts from the blacklisted/new-only sheets, score each lead:

```
Score = (Corridor_Tier × 3) + (Job_Title_Match × 2) + (Company_Size_Match × 1) + (Current_Solution_Signal × 2)

Corridor_Tier: T1=3, T2=2, T3=1
Job_Title_Match: CFO/COO/VP Ops/HR Dir=3, CEO=2, Manager=1, Other=0
Company_Size_Match: 10-50 employees=3, 50-200=2, 5-10=1, <5 or >200=0
Current_Solution_Signal: Uses Deel/Rippling=3, Uses Upwork/Fiverr=2, Uses Wise/Payoneer=1, Unknown=0
```

**Example**: Lead from UAE, CFO title, 30 employees, uses Deel → Score = 9+6+3+6 = **24/33** → TOP PRIORITY


**(25% — Sesh Iyer, Shoaib Guttoo, Khalid Kawther, etc.)**

### Data Source Strategy for EasyStaff Corridor Targeting (Mar 15, 2026)

#### The EasyStaff ICP paradox

EasyStaff helps companies **pay cross-border contractors** — people who are NOT formal employees. This means:
- Contractors DON'T list the company on LinkedIn → **Clay can't see them**
- Companies DON'T advertise "we use Pakistani contractors" on their website → **Website can't see them**
- The true ICP signal is **invisible to both data sources**

The only reliable signal: **Pakistani decision-maker in Dubai running a tech/services company** → cultural hypothesis that they have PK contractors through their network.

#### How Clay and Website scraping actually correspond

Tested 500 company domains. Of 190 where website mentions Pakistan, only 14 (7%) have Clay-confirmed PK employees. Of 30 Clay-confirmed companies, 16 had ZERO website PK signal.

**They measure DIFFERENT things:**

| Data Source | What it actually sees | What it misses |
|-------------|----------------------|----------------|
| **Clay People Search** | People on LinkedIn who list this company AND are in Pakistan = formal employees | Contractors, freelancers, part-time workers (they don't list the company) |
| **Website scraping** | What company chooses to publish: office locations, country lists, blog mentions | Private operations, contractor relationships, outsourced work |

**How to interpret each signal for EasyStaff ICP:**

| Signal | Meaning for EasyStaff | Score impact |
